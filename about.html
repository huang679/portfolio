Who I Am

Alex Rivera is a graphic designer and illustrator with 5 years of experience working with small businesses and startups. She studied Visual Communication at UT Austin and has worked with clients across food, fitness, and tech industries.

Her approach blends clean typography with hand-crafted illustration, creating work that feels both polished and personal. When she's not designing, you can find her sketching at local cafes or exploring the Texas hill country.
